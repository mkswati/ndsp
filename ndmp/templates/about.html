This project is being supported by the World Bank under the Up scaling Innovation in Disaster Risk Management Programme in Pakistan which aims to implement risk identification and financing framework that will encourage data-driven analysis and lead to a reduction in disaster risk over the long term.

The programme will test the use of evidence-based risk analysis in public policy making through a series of interventions to .

  • Provide risk information
  • Train users,
  • Re-design decision-making systems in disaster risk management
